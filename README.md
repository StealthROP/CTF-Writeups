# CTF-Writeups

Hi, I’m **Crow**, a BS Information Technology student with a strong passion for cybersecurity. My main interests are reverse engineering and binary exploitation. I enjoy breaking down systems to understand how they work at a deeper level, and using that knowledge to both exploit and secure them.

I’m also a member of **Hidden Investigations**, where I mainly handle reverse engineering and pwn challenges. Outside of CTFs, I’m interested in red teaming and exploring how systems can be tested through breaking and analyzing them.

I created this repository to help aspiring cybersecurity students, CTF players, and anyone interested in the field. Here, I document how I solved different CTF challenges, including my thought process, methodologies, scripts, and lessons learned along the way.
